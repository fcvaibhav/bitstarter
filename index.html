Features:

  1. Index any video with customizable parameters: changes in the value of these paremeters can be recorded against timestamp
  2. Extract to excel to analyse/visualize data: makes it easy to convert multimedia information to data which can then be analysed on presented in form of interactive charts
  3. Run filters to play selected parts of the clip only: instead of watching the entrie clip everytime, play the portion most relevant for your use as per the filters created
  4. Lightness of usage: Light and fast perfromance which allows easy and fast usage. Moreover, we plan to add a feature which shall facilitate the checking of potential errors.

Applications:

  1. Sports: Can be used by sports clubs, coaches, leagues, betting sites, and media companies to convert match video to data. The indexed video can be run as per custom data filter. E.g. To see the portions of the football match clip where the right back started the move "AND" it finished with a shot on goal. Or, in Tennis if you want to play only the portion where a particular player came to the net AND lost the point
  2. Retail/Marketing: Can be used to record the retail data and index it. Later on, you can customize and play with video to understand the changes in store layout in time and correlate it with sales and promotion data to study the impact of store layout
  3. Education: Indexed lectures can help in saving time of students and generating good data about an overview of how much width/deph of a particular topic in present in the video library
